Spyder Bug Liner Ski Socks for Kids

Crafted for young adventurers, these Spyder Bug Liner Ski Socks offer the perfect mix of comfort, support, and durability. Designed to keep feet warm and protected during winter sports, they are an essential addition to any kid's winter gear.

Premium Material Blend

These Bug Liner Ski Socks are made from a special combination of 72% acrylic, 25% nylon, 2% elastane, and 1% polypropylene. This blend ensures softness, breathability, and long-lasting performance, ideal for active little skiers.

Enhanced Performance Features

With compression support at the ankle and arch, these socks help improve blood circulation and provide stability during movement. The reinforced heel and toe areas are designed to withstand heavy use and maintain comfort throughout the day.

Light Cushioning for Protection

The light cushioning on the shin area adds an extra layer of protection against impact, ensuring that your child stays safe and comfortable while skiing. These socks are ideal for both casual play and more intense experiences.
