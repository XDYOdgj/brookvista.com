Helly-Hansen Junior Unisex HH LIFA Active Base Layer Set

Engineered for young adventurers, this base layer set from Helly-Hansen combines comfort, durability, and advanced moisture management to keep your child performing at their best in any weather. The LIFA Stay Dry technology at its core works tirelessly to pull moisture away from the skin, keeping them dry and warm, no matter the conditions.

With a relaxed fit that moves with them, this set offers roomy waist, thigh, knee, and cuff areas, ensuring unrestricted movement for activities like hiking, climbing, or snow play. Crafted with 130g/m2 fabric, it strikes the perfect balance between warmth and breathability, making it an ideal year-round essential for outdoor enthusiasts.

Whether you're exploring the wilderness, preparing for a camping trip, or staying active in winter sports, this base layer is designed to support comfort and performance. Its two-layer construction delivers insulation where it's needed most, while the moisture-wicking outer fabric keeps the skin dry and cool. Flat lock seams enhance comfort by eliminating chafing and creating a seamless feel against the skin.

This junior base layer set is your go-to choice for all-day wear in challenging environments. It's built to last, made to move, and designed to keep your child dry and comfortable with every step.
