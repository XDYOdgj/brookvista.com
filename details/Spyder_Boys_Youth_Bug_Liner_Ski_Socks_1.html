Spyder Boys' Youth Bug Liner Ski Socks

Designed with comfort and performance in mind, these Bug Liner Ski Socks are perfect for young skiers who want support without sacrificing style. Ideal for all-day adventures on the slopes, they keep feet warm, dry, and protected in cold weather.

Premium Materials for Superior Performance

Crafted from a high-quality blend of 72% Acrylic, 25% Nylon, 2% Elastane, and 1% Polypropylene, these socks are soft, durable, and breathable. They're built to withstand the rigors of cold-weather skiing while providing a comfortable fit.

Support Where It Matters Most

With a compression fit at the ankle and arch, these socks help improve circulation and reduce fatigue during long days on the slopes. The reinforced heel and toe areas add extra durability and protect against abrasion, ensuring they last through every trip.

Perfect for Young Skiers

Whether it's a weekend adventure or a full day of skiing, these socks are the ideal base layer. They provide warmth and support while remaining flexible enough for natural movement, keeping your child focused and comfortable all day.

Stay Dry and Cozy

Thanks to their breathable and moisture-wicking properties, these socks ensure feet stay dry and comfortable even during intense activity. The light cushioning at the shin adds an extra layer of comfort, making them perfect for long days on the mountain.

Why Choose These Socks?
  • Soft, durable, and comfortable for all-day wear
  • Breathable fabric keeps feet dry and warm in cold weather
  • Reinforced heel and toe for extra protection and strength
  • Compression fit at ankle and arch for improved performance
  • Light cushioning at the shin for added comfort on the slopes
Ideal for
  • Young skiers seeking support and style
  • Morning slopes or long days of winter activity
  • Ski enthusiasts who value comfort and durability
