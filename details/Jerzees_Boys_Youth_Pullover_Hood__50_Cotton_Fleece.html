Jerzees Boys' Youth Pullover Hood

Crafted for comfort and style, this hoodie is made with a premium blend of 50% cotton and 50% polyester. The soft, preshrunk fleece offers a cozy feel that's perfect for everyday wear, whether your child is heading to school, playing sports, or relaxing at home.

With a high-stitch density, the fabric provides a smooth canvas for vibrant print designs, while the double-needle coverseaming ensures durability and a clean, professional look. The 1x1 ribbed cuffs and waistband add a snug fit and enhanced elasticity, keeping the hoodie in place all day long. A concealed seam on the cuffs enhances the sleek, stylish appearance of this versatile piece.

This hoodie is designed to adapt to any occasion, making it a great choice for layering in cooler weather or wearing on its own for a casual, everyday style. Parents will appreciate the attention to detail and reliable construction that ensures long-lasting comfort and quality.

Key Features

  • 50% Cotton & 50% Polyester NuBlend for Soft Comfort
  • Preshrunk Fleece with High-Stitch Density for Smooth Printing
  • Double-Needle Coverseaming for Durable Fit
  • 1x1 Ribbed Cuffs and Waistband for Better Shape Retention
  • Concealed Seam on Cuffs for Sleek Look

Why Choose This Hoodie?

Ideal for casual wear and layering, this hoodie offers a perfect balance of style and comfort. The soft fleece material keeps your child warm without sacrificing breathability, while the durable construction ensures it lasts through active play and daily use.
