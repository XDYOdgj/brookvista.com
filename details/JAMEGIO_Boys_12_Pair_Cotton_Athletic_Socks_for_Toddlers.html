JAMEGIO Boys' Crew Socks

These JAMEGIO boys' crew socks are made with a premium blend of 75% cotton, 20% polyester, and 5% spandex. The soft and breathable material ensures all-day comfort while keeping little feet cool and dry, making them ideal for toddlers and growing boys. Designed for everyday wear and active play, they offer a perfect fit that moves with your child’s activity, without any irritation.

Key Features

  • Soft and Durable Fabric: Combines 75% cotton for a gentle feel and 20% polyester for long-lasting wear. The 5% spandex adds flexibility and comfort during play.
  • 3 Sizes for Every Growing Step: Available in 2-4T, 4-6 Years, and 6-8 Years to ensure a perfect fit for all ages.
  • 12 Pairs in 6 Vibrant Colors: Offer variety and easy matching for any outfit, perfect for casual or athletic occasions.
  • Reinforced Toes and Heels: Designed for long-lasting comfort and durability, reducing wear and tear during active use.
  • Moisture-Wicking & Breathable: Keeps feet dry and comfortable, even during the hottest days or longest play sessions.

Why Choose These Socks?

Whether your child is in school, playing sports, or just exploring the world, these socks are the perfect addition to their wardrobe. The elastic design ensures they stay in place, while the reinforced areas add extra support and strength. Ideal for toddlers and active boys, they provide support, comfort, and style all in one essential piece of apparel.

Perfect for Every Occasion
