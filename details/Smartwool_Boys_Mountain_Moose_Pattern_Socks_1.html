Smartwool Boys Mountain Moose Pattern Socks

Designed for active little adventurers, these Smartwool socks combine comfort with a playful mountain moose design that keeps kids entertained and stylish all day long.

Key Features
  • Full cushioning in the leg and foot for all-day comfort
  • Flat knit toe seam reduces irritation and chafing
  • Moose design adds fun and charm to outdoor gear
  • Suitable for toddlers through little big kids
  • Breathable and durable for active play and movement
Why Choose These Socks?

These socks are ideal for children who spend time outdoors, at play, or even at bedtime.
